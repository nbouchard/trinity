Headings with Subtext

Add subtext to a heading by including a <span> inside <h1>, <h2>, or <h3>. Contents of the <span> will be treated as subtext.

Heading One Subtext


Heading Two Subtext


Heading Three Subtext 

<h1>
    Heading One
    <span>Subtext</span>
</h1>

<h2>
    Heading Two
    <span>Subtext</span>
</h2>

<h3>
    Heading Three
    <span>Subtext</span>
</h3>
