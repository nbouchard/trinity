Energy Suppliers

The power to choose is yours

Over 13 states allow you to shop around for energy plans and compare prices from competing suppliers.

If you’re a bit fuzzy on what an energy supplier is and how a supplier differs from a utility, you’re not alone. Suppliers sell you the actual energy that you use to turn on your lights and fire up your stove. Utilities, on the other hand, take the energy from the supplier and deliver it to your door - just like the post office does with an online purchase.

Because there are so many suppliers out there, looking for a plan can feel overwhelming. That's where Choose Energy can help. We only feature supplier plans with no teaser rates or hidden fees. We do all the work for you by partnering with only the most reputable suppliers - check them out below.
