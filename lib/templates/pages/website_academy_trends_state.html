Local insights to empower your local energy decisions. As your energy advocate, Choose Energy analyzes hundreds of thousands of energy consumer data points each month to provide you with accurate and real-time trends in a simple format.

September is the third most expensive month for con-ed customers because they use 28% more electricity compared to an average month. Customers can take advantage of a forecasted 8% decrease in energy prices to offset their higher consumption.

Nearly 75% of New Yorkers select plans longer than 12 months, demonstrating local interest in fixed prices to avoid price volatility.

New Yorkers prefer lower priced, long term plans, rather than paying a premium for green energy plans. And there are many great low rate, 12 month plans now offered on ChooseEnergy.com

Con-ed customers looking to avoid price volatility are coming to Choose Energy in high volumes to secure fixed rates, as rates tend to actually peak for con-ed customers in the fall months.
