    Frequently Asked Questions

    • When will this plan start?

      After selecting your plan and accepted by your selected supplier after completing the enrollment form, your new plan will typically begin on the next applicable meter read date after your utility processes your enrollment and continue for the number of billing cycles (months) listed in the plan contract. The account migration process can take 30 - 60 days depending on your utility's and selected supplier's enrollment process and meter read date schedule.

    • What happens when this plan ends?

      One of two things: Either the contract converts to a month-to-month plan at the default fixed/variable market rate, or the plan will be renewed for a second term at the default fixed/variable market rate. Consult your plan's documents for details. Choose Energy and your new supplier will notify you 30-60 days before your plan ends.

    • How will changing suppliers impact my bill?

      The plan you choose will only impact the charges on the supply portion of your bill. You will continue to be billed by your utility for the distribution and delivery of your energy.
