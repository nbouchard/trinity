Diversity

Choose Energy is committed to diversity of thought on all of our teams. We are helping people of all walks of life make better choices around their energy purchases, and a team comprised of people with differing experiences will lead to a better workplace, a better product, and a better result for our customers. We especially want to encourage individuals with unique life experiences and people of all genders, ethnicities, ages, and lifestyles to apply.
